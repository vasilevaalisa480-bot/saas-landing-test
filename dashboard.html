How to integrate a form on your website

Point your HTML form at the Leadoscope ingest endpoint. Use POST with application/x-www-form-urlencoded or application/json (see API reference in your contract). Always collect explicit consent for medical contact where required. 

<form action="https://api.leadoscope.example/v1/clinics/YOUR_CLINIC_ID/leads"
      method="post">
  <input type="hidden" name="api_key" value="YOUR_PUBLIC_FORM_KEY" />
  <label>Full name <input name="patient_name" required /></label>
  <label>Phone <input name="phone" type="tel" required /></label>
  <label>Service <select name="service">…</select></label>
  <label>
    <input type="checkbox" name="consent" value="1" required />
    I agree to be contacted about my appointment.
  </label>
  <button type="submit">Request appointment</button>
</form>

Required fields:

  • patient_name — full name as it should appear in the CRM.
  • phone — primary contact number for reminders.
  • service — service line or reason for visit (maps to routing rules).
  • consent — affirmative consent flag for outreach (checkbox or equivalent).
